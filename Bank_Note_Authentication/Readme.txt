About the dataset:
 
The task is to classify the note as real or fake , 0 being fake and 1 being real.
You can find the dataset over here.https://www.kaggle.com/ritesaluja/bank-note-authentication-uci-data

Algorithm used:

Logistic Regression
K-Nearest Neighbors
Kernel SVM
Naive Bayes
Decision Tree Classifier
Random Forest Classifier

The Best Models are Kernel SVM and K-Nearest Neighbors with an accuracy of 100% .
